Why is Jujutsu Kaisen peak anime?

Jujutsu Kaisen has quickly risen to prominence in the anime world, captivating audiences globally with its unique blend of compelling storytelling, exhilarating action, and deep emotional resonance. Created by Gege Akutami, the series is widely regarded as one of the best new-generation anime, rivaling classics with its well-rounded appeal. Here are some reasons why Jujutsu Kaisen stands out as "peak" anime.

Characters

Unlike many protagonists who follow a rigid moral code, Yuji grapples with morally complex decisions, especially in his journey to rid the world of curses. His relationships with fellow sorcerers, like the introspective Megumi Fushiguro and the fierce Nobara Kugisaki, add depth to his character, and each team member has a unique, fully realized personality. This ensemble cast adds richness to the story, making every interaction feel significant.
